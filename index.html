Privacy Notice

This app is a voice-powered teleprompter. To work, it listens to your voice through your browser's built-in speech recognition.

What you should know: your voice audio is sent to Google (if you're using Chrome, Edge, or most other browsers) or Apple (if you're using Safari) to be turned into text. That's how the app knows where you are in your script. This happens through your browser, not through us — we never receive your voice or see what you say.

If you're not okay with Google or Apple processing your voice, please don't use this app. There's no version that avoids it.

You can read Google's Privacy Policy here and Apple's Privacy Policy here.

What we do with your data: nothing. We don't collect your voice, your transcripts, or the scripts you load. Everything stays in your browser.

About the site itself: this app is hosted on GitHub Pages, so GitHub logs basic visitor info like IP addresses for security purposes. That's standard for any website and we don't have access to it.
